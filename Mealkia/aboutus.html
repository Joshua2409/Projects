About Mealkia


First Meal Service in Indonesia that serve different kind of cuisine from all over the nation.


Discover the World of Flavors at Your Home with Mealkia


Welcome to Mealkia, your gateway to a culinary adventure that combines convenience, affordability, and the vibrant flavors of Indonesia. We are a meal delivery service based in Indonesia, committed to providing you with delicious recipes and high-quality ingredients right at your doorstep.

At Mealkia, we understand the joy of cooking and the importance of using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Our aim is to showcase the rich diversity of cuisines around the world with the focus of exploring Indonesian Cuisine while promoting sustainable food practices. By prioritizing local ingredients, we support local farmers and reduce our carbon footprint, all while delivering exceptional meals to your table.



Our Commitment to Quality



We are passionate about food, and our team of culinary experts meticulously curates each recipe to ensure an authentic and unforgettable dining experience. From traditional classics to innovative fusions, our recipes are designed to introduce you to the incredible flavors the world. To guarantee the highest quality, we work closely with local farmers, fishermen, and suppliers who share our commitment to excellence. We source our ingredients directly from these partners, ensuring their freshness and supporting local communities. For those rare ingredients that are not readily available, we import them to maintain the integrity of our recipes and provide you with an authentic culinary experience.



Convenience and Affordability



We understand that busy lifestyles can make it challenging to find time for cooking elaborate meals. That's why Mealkia is here to make your life easier. Our meal kits come with pre-portioned ingredients and easy-to-follow recipes, allowing you to create a delicious, homemade meal without the hassle of grocery shopping or extensive meal planning. We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality, delicious meals at affordable prices. Mealkia offers competitive pricing, making our meal kits accessible to a wide range of individuals and families. We are committed to delivering exceptional value while ensuring that the authenticity and quality of our ingredients are never compromised.



Join the Mealkia Community



Mealkia is more than just a meal delivery service; it's a community of food enthusiasts, passionate about exploring the diverse flavors of the World. We invite you to join our community and embark on a culinary journey like no other. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a novice in the kitchen, Mealkia is here to inspire you, elevate your cooking skills, and satisfy your taste buds. Stay connected with us through our website and social media platforms, where you'll find a wealth of culinary tips, tricks, and stories about cuisines of the world. We also love hearing from our customers, so feel free to share your feedback, suggestions, and recipe requests. Your input helps us continuously improve our services and bring you the best possible dining experience.

Join us on this culinary adventure and let Mealkia be your trusted companion in exploring the rich tapestry of world's best flavors. Order your first meal kit today and unlock a world of mouthwatering possibilities.
