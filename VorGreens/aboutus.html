VOrgreens


VOrgreens was founded in 2011 with the aim of bringing a new dimension to healthy eatery and approach to fast food. Today’s society is becoming increasingly disconnected with their food – we want to show that food consumption should be honest, trustworthy and real. As we’ve grown over the past few years, we constantly strive to improve and embrace an attitude to be “better than yesterday”.



We currently operate in Singapore, Philippines, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Japan, Spain, Korea, Vietnam and Malaysia. We aim to become the world’s leading nutrition, health and wellness company serving the best tasting, most nutritious food. As we expand our footprint in the region, we remain true to our core values and commitment to the ideology of “Eat Wide Awake” through all aspects.



As we set up the salad bar every morning, we are driven by the communities that we work with and the lives that we are able to impact. We are just at the beginning of an amazing journey and dream about a world where farmers are respected and customers can start trusting their food again. We are still a small company but with big dreams and hope that we can be one of the catalysts in this change.

"The way you think, the way you behave, the way you eat, can influence your life by 30 to 50 years." — Deepak Chopra.
